Trends

Q1 continued the strong momentum from Q4, with MAU crossing 1.2M. Growth was driven by: 1) word-of-mouth from AI assistant features; 2) the Notion integration launch bringing new users.

Paid conversion improved from 2.83% to 3.28%, primarily due to the new pricing strategy and clearer upgrade prompts.

  • A 15-minute service outage in mid-February caused a 35% drop in daily signups. Root cause fixed (database connection pool misconfiguration).
  • Japan market showed abnormal growth in March (+120%), traced to a YouTube influencer recommendation.

Segments

By User Type

Individual users account for 72%, team users 28%. Team users have 4.5x higher ARPU and represent the key growth focus.

By Region

Domestic market 55%, North America 25%, Europe 12%, others 8%. Japan jumped from 2% to 5% in March.

By Feature

AI assistant is the most popular new feature, used by 68% of weekly active users. Real-time collaboration at 42% and rising.

Methodology

Data source: Internal data warehouse (Snowflake). Metrics consistent with prior quarter definitions. MAU = users with at least one login in the month. Paid users = users with active subscription during the period.
