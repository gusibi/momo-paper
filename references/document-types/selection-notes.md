# Document Type Selection Notes

Use this file when the user's words are close to multiple document types.

## Common tie-breakers

- `one_pager` vs `long_doc`
  - choose `one_pager` when the output should fit a short executive read
  - choose `long_doc` when the reader needs a developed argument or report-like structure

- `portfolio` vs `case_study`
  - choose `portfolio` when multiple pieces of work need curation
  - choose `case_study` when one project needs a full breakdown

- `research_summary` vs `stats_report`
  - choose `research_summary` when the user wants conclusion-first interpretation
  - choose `stats_report` when they want dense KPI and trend reporting

- `equity_report` vs `research_summary`
  - choose `equity_report` when the content revolves around a single stock, valuation, catalysts, or price history
  - choose `research_summary` when the subject is broader and not security-specific

If two choices still feel plausible after these checks, ask a one-line scenario question.
