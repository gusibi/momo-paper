Business Snapshot

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. The company also sells a variety of related services, including AppleCare, cloud services, digital content, and payment services.

As of Q1 FY2025, Apple has an installed base of over 2.2 billion active devices, providing a powerful platform for services monetization and ecosystem lock-in.

Price and Drivers

Over the past 12 months, AAPL has traded in a range of $165-$225, currently at $198. The stock has benefited from resilient iPhone demand and accelerating services growth, though regulatory concerns in the EU and China remain overhangs.

Key catalysts for the next 12 months include: AI feature rollout across device lineup, Vision Pro ecosystem expansion, and potential new product categories.

  • AI integration across iOS, macOS, and iPadOS driving upgrade cycle
  • Services revenue approaching $100B annually with expanding margins
  • Vision Pro developer ecosystem growing 40% QoQ
  • India and Southeast Asia emerging as key growth markets
